Delivered a talk to clinicians and researchers in the Emory University Department of Orthopedics on recent advances and emerging opportunities in joint acoustic emissions (JAE) for musculoskeletal assessment. The presentation highlighted the potential of JAE as a non-invasive sensing modality, current challenges in signal acquisition and interpretation, and future directions for machine learning–driven analysis in clinical settings. This talk was given in collaboration with Dr. Ruchik Mishra and Chuoqi Chen.
