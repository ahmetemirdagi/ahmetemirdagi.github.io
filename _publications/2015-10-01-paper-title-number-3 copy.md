 To facilitate the manual annotation process of SCG signals, we developed an open-source graphical user interface, providing a simple, efficient, and accurate tool. Using data from a porcine hypovolemia protocol, we annotated SCG waveforms for signal quality scores and annotated cardiac timing intervals with 17059 SCG heart beats.
