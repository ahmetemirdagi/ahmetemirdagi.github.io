I am a Ph.D. student at the Georgia Institute of Technology and a graduate researcher in the Inan Research Lab, where I work under the supervision of [Prof. Omer T. Inan](https://ece.gatech.edu/directory/omer-t-inan). 

My research focuses on deep learning methods for joint acoustic emissions, with a particular emphasis on knee acoustic signals. More broadly, I am interested in efficient implementation of deep learning frameworks for edge-device deployment in wearable healthcare, as well as foundation models, especially multimodal fusion for cardiovascular signals.
